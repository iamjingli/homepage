About Me

I joined Tianjin University as an Assistant Professor at the College of Intelligence and Computing in 2018. Previously, I received the B.S. degree and M.Sc. degree from Heilongjiang University in 2011 and 2014, and the Ph.D. degree from Dalian University of Technology under the supervision of Prof.Peihua Li in 2018. My research is on computer vision and deep learning, particularly visual classification and deep architectures based on probability distribution modeling or high-order statistical modeling.
